01 · Research

Questions I care about

01 / Self-reference

Self-Referential AI

I study AI systems that learn by reasoning over their own outputs. Inspired by Hofstadter’s strange loops and Schmidhuber’s Gödel Machine, I view self-reward, self-play, self-distillation, and recursive self-improvement as expressions of a shared principle: models using self-reference to reshape their behavior.

02 / Emergence

Multi-Agent Emergence

Inspired by Anderson’s “more is different” and Wolfram’s A New Kind of Science, I study emergence in multi-agent AI: how simple local rules, game-theoretic interactions, and large agent populations give rise to collective intelligence that no individual agent possesses.
