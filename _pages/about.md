I'm a graduate student at **MIT**, pursuing an M.S. in [**Computational Science and Engineering (CSE)**](https://cse.mit.edu/) and an M.S. in [**Technology and Policy (TPP)**](https://tpp.mit.edu/). I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Physics from [**Tsinghua University**](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/) in 2025.
